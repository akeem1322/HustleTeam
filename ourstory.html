Our Story

The Dreamers Company is a brand built from ground zero. We embody the passion, hustle, and grit of life through our clothing.


The brand was founded in 2013 in Richmond,VA and was built around Ambition, Dream-chasing, and Prosperity. Our purpose is to inspire others, spread positivity, and change the world through clothing, community renovation, and education.


With us, clothing is one piece to the puzzle. We have a mission to use profit to empower communities with need base businesses , proper real estate lining and quality education to underprivileged children.
